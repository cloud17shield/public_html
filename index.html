Real-time Drivers’ Behavior Anomaly Detection

About our project

Car-hailing platforms, such as Didi Chuxing and Uber are very popular in mainland China, which greatly facilitates people's travel. However, due to the lack of supervision and the difficulty of supervision, the personal safety of drivers and passengers can not be guaranteed. For example, drivers may be drunk, tired or distracted while driving, which threatens the safety of passengers. robbery of passengers by drivers and robbery of drivers by passengers are not uncommon.

So we try to solve this problem by trying to design and implement a drivers’ anomaly detection system to recognize drunk driving, drowsy driving, distracted driving and danger objects in real time based on Kubernetes.The driver's face and peripheral images will be captured by the camera of his mobile phone, and then transmitted to the background system based on cloud through the network. The system will process and judge the images in real time. If there are drunk driving, fatigue driving, distracted driving or dangerous objects, the alarm will be issued.
